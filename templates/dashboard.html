Follow this weekly program to build muscle memory and improve posture scores.

Day 1: Stance & Agility 20 Mins

Practice basic shadow movements and low-ready court positioning drills.

Day 2: Overhead Clears 30 Mins

Focus on full shoulder rotation and 90-120° elbow extensions at hitting frame.

Day 3: Smash Technique 25 Mins

Practice core pivot rotation and fast wrist snap actions for velocity.

Day 4: Net Play & Drops 20 Mins

Work on soft touch net drops and tight wrist control at the tape.

Day 5: Simulation & Footwork 35 Mins

Combine shadow play with full-court movement drills and high-intensity intervals.
